About Balad NG

Leading the future of global trade with innovation and excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Balad NG has grown from a small regional importer to a global trade powerhouse. Our journey began with a simple vision: to make international trade accessible to businesses of all sizes.

Today, we operate in over 50 countries, connecting suppliers with markets and creating opportunities for growth and expansion. Our innovative approach to logistics and supply chain management has revolutionized the industry.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients, understanding their unique needs, and delivering solutions that exceed expectations. Our team of experts brings decades of experience in international trade, customs regulations, and market analysis.
